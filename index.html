Open Positions

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Data Science at UVA. I lead the Aikyam lab and am the founder of Agyeya Artificial IQ Foundation, which focuses on democratizing research by supporting open collaboration in ML research and training the next generation of AI students in India. What inspires me to do what we do is the feeling of helping the community through my work! Feel free to connect with me if you are excited about Trustworthy AI research or its applications.

I am recruiting students for Fall 2025. Please check Open Positions for more details and my mentoring philosophy. For more details about me and my research, please check out my CV.

Welcome to Aikyam Lab (Sanskrit: ऐक्यम्; meaning "oneness")! We focus on developing Trustworthy Machine Learning Frameworks that go beyond training models for specific downstream tasks and satisfy trustworthy properties, such as explainability, fairness, and robustness.
